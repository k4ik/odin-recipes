Chicken and Avocado Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper. Grill or cook the chicken in a skillet over medium heat until cooked through. Let it cool, then slice it into thin strips.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, sliced avocado, and red onion.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Add the sliced chicken to the salad bowl and drizzle the dressing over the salad.
  5. Toss the salad gently until all the ingredients are coated with the dressing.
  6. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve the Chicken and Avocado Salad immediately and enjoy!
