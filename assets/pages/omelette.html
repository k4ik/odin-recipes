Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until well blended. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the spinach and red bell pepper to the skillet and saut√© for about 2 minutes until slightly softened.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet, making sure the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  5. Cook the omelette for about 2-3 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly moist.
  6. Sprinkle the grated cheddar cheese over the omelette and fold it in half.
  7. Cook for another 1 minute, or until the cheese melts.
  8. Remove the omelette from the heat and transfer it to a plate.
  9. Garnish with halved cherry tomatoes on top.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
